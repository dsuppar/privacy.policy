# Privacy Policy Last updated: June 8, 2026 Coppola Boat Club ("CBC", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Coppola Boat Club mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The app is a private, invitation-only application used by a small group of friends, available exclusively to members with a valid invitation code. It is not operated by a registered company or organisation. ## 1. Data Controller Coppola Boat Club is a private, non-commercial app maintained by a private individual. It is not a registered company or organisation and has no commercial purpose. Contact: [coppolaboatclub@outlook.com](mailto:coppolaboatclub@outlook.com) ## 2. Data We Collect When you create an account and use the Coppola Boat Club app, we collect the following information: • Account information: Email address and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication). • Profile information: Display name shown to other members (e.g. in chat and bookings). • Location data (including background location): The app automatically records your sailing route and sailing time while you are out on the water — including when the screen is off. We never track your location while you are on land, and location data is never shared with third parties for marketing. • Activity data: Bookings and reservations, chat messages, and fuel/trip records you create in the app. • Usage data: Basic app interaction data collected by Firebase to ensure the service functions correctly. ## 3. How We Use Your Data We use your data exclusively to: • Create and manage your account. • Show you bookings, messages, and trip records. • Record your sailing route and sailing time while you are sailing. • Send push notifications about relevant activity (e.g. bookings and messages). • Maintain security and prevent unauthorised access. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. ## 4. Legal Basis (GDPR) We process your data on the following legal grounds: • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing your email and display name is necessary to provide the service you signed up for. • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Location tracking is enabled with your explicit permission and can be revoked at any time in your device settings. • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): Keeping records of bookings and trips to ensure fair and accurate club management. ## 5. Third-Party Services Coppola Boat Club uses the following third-party infrastructure services to operate: • Device location services (Apple Location Services): Your location is collected via your device's built-in GPS, handled by the operating system. The app only accesses it after you grant permission, and you can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings. • Apple Maps (Apple MapKit): Used to display the map and your recorded sailing route on your device. Map rendering is provided by Apple. • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore): Stores your account credentials, profile data, and activity data — including your recorded sailing routes. Data may be stored in EU data centres. See Google's Privacy Policy at privacy.google.com. • Open-Meteo (weather data): Used to retrieve sailing weather forecasts. The app requests weather for a fixed location (the club's sailing area) only — your personal location is never sent to this service. • Apple Push Notification service (APNs): Used to deliver push notifications to your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Your location data is never shared with any third party for marketing, advertising, or tracking purposes. ## 6. Data Retention Your account and profile data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account directly in the app under Profile → Delete account. When you delete your account, the following is removed: • Your profile (email and display name) and your login credentials. • Your chat messages. Certain data created during your membership — such as bookings, fuel records, and trip/sailing records — is necessary for the club's shared operational records and may be retained indefinitely, even after your account is deleted. Where possible, such records are kept without your personal profile attached. If you wish to have this remaining data removed as well, you can request it by contacting us at [coppolaboatclub@outlook.com](mailto:coppolaboatclub@outlook.com), and we will remove it within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. ## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR, you have the right to: Access Request a copy of your personal data. Rectification Correct inaccurate data via your profile settings. Erasure Request deletion of your account, profile, and chat messages (available directly in the app). Other records may be retained as described in section 6. Portability Receive your data in a portable format. Objection Object to processing based on legitimate interests. Restriction Request that we limit processing of your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [coppolaboatclub@outlook.com](mailto:coppolaboatclub@outlook.com). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Authority ([datatilsynet.dk](https://www.datatilsynet.dk/)). ## 8. Children's Privacy Coppola Boat Club is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly. ## 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. ## 10. Contact For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at [coppolaboatclub@outlook.com](mailto:coppolaboatclub@outlook.com).
